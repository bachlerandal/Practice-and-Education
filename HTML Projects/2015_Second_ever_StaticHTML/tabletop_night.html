Upcoming TableTop Night! 22/Nov/2015!!!!!

This sunday (22 Nov 2015) will be our first ever recorded TableTop Night. We will be joining Evan, Tommy, Ben, and their friends Joey and Joe (confusing I know) as they roleplay their way through a fantasy world that they have already thrown into chaos. In past adventures, our heros have pillaged their way across the countryside, killed kings (apparently they always roll 20s when committing regicide), and thrown half a continent into civil war. With the goal of killing a fictional enemy in the most ridiculous way possible always at the forefront of their battle plans, hilarity inevitably ensues. Grappling hook throw over a mob of enemies to latch onto their commander who is leading them from behind? Natural 20.



Future Tabletop nights will include hidden gems in the board game world. Our calender currently has Lords of Waterdeep, Settlers of Catan, Dominion, and King of Tokyo penciled in. Stay tuned, content is on its way.
